How I plan my year

so i dont waste a year of my life

by ahmad fahmy / @fahmyboy

background

I have been planning my year in advance for the past 5 years. this is the sum total of my experience.

  1. review previous years
  2. braindump
  3. discuss with wife and kids
  4. create synergizes
  5. visualize

year-end review

review previous years list and make note of what you accomplished and why

5 whys are a greate technique to get to the underlyng cause of why you didnt finish something...

I usually notice trends. e.g. i accomplish all my work goals but very few of my spiritual goals

the brain dump

click the down arrow for more

i spend the month of december just listing out random thoughts of of what I want to accomplish.

I currently use google keep but you can try a pen and paper

I review the brain dump with my wife and kids and get there oppinions

categorize and calenderize

I take all the items from the brain dump and categorize them

  • fitness
  • family
  • travel
  • kids
  • career

wife review

I usually have very aggressive fitness goals and my wife has travel goals. In order to create a win-win I try and select races in locations she wants to visit

if there are ny goals that are selfish or will require a lot of time, I will get her permission to do those

visualize

I print out my goals and put them everywhere where I can see them
